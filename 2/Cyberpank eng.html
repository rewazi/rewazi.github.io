Cyberpunk 2077

About the Game

"Cyberpunk 2077" is an action-RPG by CD Projekt RED, where players immerse themselves in the vast world of Night City—a future metropolis filled with everything from futuristic technology to criminal conflicts. Dive into the cyberpunk atmosphere and uncover the secrets of this amazing world!

Features

  • Genre: Action, RPG
  • Developer: CD Projekt RED
  • Platforms: PC, PS5, Xbox Series X
  • Rating: 8.7/10
