GTA 5

About the Game

"GTA 5" is an open-world action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. The game allows players to immerse themselves in the world of Los Santos and its surroundings, controlling three main characters. You can conduct heists, participate in races, and complete various other missions, enjoying the open world and freedom of action.

Features

  • Genre: Action, Adventure
  • Developer: Rockstar North
  • Platforms: PC, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X
  • Rating: 8.5/10
