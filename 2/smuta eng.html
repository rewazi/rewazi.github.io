Smuta

About the Game

"Smuta" is a historical action game that immerses the player in the era of the Time of Troubles in Russia. You will have to endure challenging times and participate in events that shaped the country's history. Dive into the atmosphere of the past and uncover the secrets of this historical period!

Features

  • Genre: Historical Action
  • Developer: Historical Games
  • Platforms: PC, PS5, Xbox Series X
  • Rating: 8.0/10
