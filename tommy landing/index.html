Thomas Tammettes was born on
November 18, 1991, into a poor family. The
father spoke Russian, and the mother
spoke Estonian. The boy lived with his
parents in a Khrushchevka on the
outskirts of Tallinn, in one of the poorest
and most criminal areas of the city —
Kopli.

-WE LIVED IN A ONE-ROOM APARTMENT, THIS ROOM
